This web application is trained to recognise different types of Brain Tumours from Brain MRI images.
The algorithm achieved accuracy exceeding 99.80% on the relevant test set of more than 1300 brain MRI images.
Please note that the predictive accuracy of the model depends on the quality and quantity of the training data.
Therefore the model has limitations and cannot be used for definite clinical predictions. Instead it can be used for verification and research purposes.
